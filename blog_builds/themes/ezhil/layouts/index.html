Hi, I'm Sachita, and I study how recommender systems shape the news ecosystem! Welcome to my homepage on the interwebz!

I'm currently looking for research internships for Summer 2023!

I'm a 3rd-year PhD student at Northwestern University's program in Technology and Social Behaviour. I work with Dr. Nick Diakopoulos in the Computational Journalism Lab.

In my work, I design recommender systems to assist journalists and newsrooms with different tasks, and I study their impacts on the news that is produced. My projects are situated at the crossroads of HCI, Machine Learning, and Communication Studies. Ultimately, I want to design and study algorithmic tools that priortize human values, user autonomy, and transparency.
