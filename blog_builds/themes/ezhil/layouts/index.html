Hi, I'm Sachita, and I design information retrieval systems to help science/technology journalists discover + understand newsworthy stories from the latest scientific research. This task is often difficult for journalists because large repositories of science articles can be difficult and time-consuming to sift through to unearth good quality stories. I approach this research from a human-centered perspective, by trying to co-design with journalists and conducting mixed-methods work. I thus draw from methods in HCI, natural language processing, and machine learning.

I'm currently looking for research internships for Summer 2023!

I'm a 3rd-year PhD student at NU's program in Technology and Social Behaviour. I'm advised by Dr. Nick Diakopoulos in the Computational Journalism Lab.
