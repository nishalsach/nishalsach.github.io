Hi, I'm Sachita, a 4th-year PhD in human-computer interaction and AI @ Northwestern University advised by Dr. Nick Diakopoulos. I design human-centered AI systems that support sense-making and creativity for news reporters as they engage with swathes of information.

In particular, I design systems to support science journalists as they discover, understand, and brainstorm newsworthy stories. I endeavor to align these systems with journalists' professional and ethical values, as well as to center user agency, contextual flexibility, and AI accountability.

I have presented my work at HCI/AI conferences like CSCW, CHI, DIS, and FAccT, as well as at journalism conferences like Big Local News and SRCCON.

You can email me at: nishal [at] u.northwestern.edu
