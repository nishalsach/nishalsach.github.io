Hi, I'm Sachita, and I build interactive, explainable AI-infused systems for news discovery and recommendation. I'm a 3rd-year PhD @ Northwestern University, where I'm advised by Dr. Nick Diakopoulos. Before this, I completed my undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Engineering at BITS Pilani's beautiful Goa campus.

I draw on approaches from HCI, NLP and ML to build explainable, interactive AI systems that journalists can use to discover + make sense of potentially newsworthy stories, in service of broader decision-making about what makes the news. I have presented my work at conferences such as CSCW, CHI, ICWSM, (and soon will at) FAccT.

Here's my email: nishal [at] u.northwestern.edu
