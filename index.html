Hi, I'm Sachita, and I design information retrieval systems to help science/technology journalists explore and draw inspiration from the expanding landscape of scientific research, to better support their news reporting. I approach this research from a human-centered perspective, drawing from methods in HCI and natural language processing.

I'm currently looking for research internships for Summer 2023!

I'm a 3rd-year PhD student at NU's program in Technology and Social Behaviour. I'm advised by Dr. Nick Diakopoulos in the Computational Journalism Lab.
