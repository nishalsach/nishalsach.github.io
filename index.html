    Hi, I'm Sachita, and I build interactive, user-centered systems to support human decision-making. I'm a 3rd-year PhD @ Northwestern Univeristy, where I'm advised by Dr. Nick Diakopoulos.

    I use methods in HCI and ML to build systems to help journalists discover + understand newsworthy stories, to support decision-making about what makes the news. Ultimately, I want to design algorithmic tools that prioritize user autonomy, accountability and transparency.

    Here's my email: nishal at u.northwestern.edu
