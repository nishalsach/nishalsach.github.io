    Hi, I'm Sachita, a 4th-year PhD @ Northwestern University advised by Dr. Nick Diakopoulos. I design interactive, intelligent interfaces that leverage NLP and ML to support journalists in their search, sense-making, and decision-making during news production.

    My research focuses on centering user autonomy, contextual flexibility, and algorithmic intelligbility when journalists interface with AI. I've presented my work at conferences like CSCW, CHI, DIS, and FAccT.

    I'm looking for research internships for Summer 2024. Please get in touch if my work sounds relevant/interesting/fun!

    You can email me at: nishal [at] u.northwestern.edu
